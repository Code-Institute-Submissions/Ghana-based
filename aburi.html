Aburi Adventure

Aburi Botanical Gardens and Quadbiking (Half day)

This an amazing half day adventure in Aburi as you get to discover some greenery Ghana has to offer! First half of the day will be spent at the Aburi Botanical garden where you'll have a chilled stroll through, catch some rays as well as being some nature. As for the next half, we've teamed up with ATV Aburi to get you a discounted ticketed tour on quadbike! You'll be able to check off riding in the mountains off that bucket list! ITINERARY

Aburi Botanical Gardens visit
Arrival at midday, our in house tour guide will be ready to show you around right from the entrance. As beautiful as the botanical gardens are, there is a lot of history dating as far back as the 1800s; from the carved tree to the abandoned helicopter. You'll be able to have a look at some local plants unique to Ghana as well as other gifted ones. There is also a cafe in the garden where we'll be able to take a break and relax a little before the next half.

Quadbiking in the mountains!
There are very few places that offer quadbiking in the mountains but Ghana is just the gift that keeps on giving! You'll be kitted out with a safety helmet upon arrival as well as allocated your own bike. Expect all the adrenaline alongside the best views.
