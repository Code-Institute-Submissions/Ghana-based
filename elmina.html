Elmina Escape

Kakum National Park adventure, Elmina Castle history tour and Elmina Beach (Full day)

Elmina is a must when you come to Ghana because of all these attractions. Early morning pick up to avoid the Accra traffic and get you ready to explore Kakum National Park -home of the 3rd largest canopy walkway in Africa. It consists of 7 bridges which increase in height as you go along, which level will you reach? You'll be greeted by your tour guide at Elmina Castle who will tell about how it contributed to the big dark history of Ghana. This package also includes all transits
